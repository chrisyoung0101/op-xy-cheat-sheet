Teenage Engineering OP‑XY Cheat Sheet

Quick reference for common actions and shortcuts

Pattern Operations

Action Shortcut
Add a bar SHIFT + BAR
Remove a bar SHIFT + BAR
Duplicate current bar SHIFT + BAR
Set pattern length HOLD BAR → rotate white encoder
Create new pattern ARRANGE → M1
Copy/paste pattern ARRANGE → M2, move, M3
Delete pattern ARRANGE → M4
Scroll through patterns ARRANGE → rotate white encoder

Step Editing

Basic Step Actions

  • Copy/Paste a step: HOLD source step → TAP destination
  • Nudge a step off-grid: Hold step → press L or R
  • Tie/extend note: Hold start & end steps → press SHIFT
  • Add step component: Hold step + component key

Pattern Controls

  • Set pattern length: HOLD BAR → SELECT desired last step → RELEASE
  • Step-record mode: Hold RECORD → enter steps
  • Live record mode: RECORD + PLAY → perform steps

Recording & Automation

Action Shortcut
Parameter lock Hold step → twist knob
Record knob automation Hold RECORD → turn knob
Smooth automation Hold BAR → turn white encoder
Undo last edit SHIFT + RECORD
Erase current track Hold RECORD + STOP
Punch-in FX live SHIFT + Fx key
Sequence punch-in FX AUX → FX track → record

Track & FX Controls

Track Mix

  • Mute/unmute track: MIX → hold SHIFT + track button
  • Quick mute in Arrange: Click white encoder on track
  • Adjust track volume: MIX → rotate white encoder

FX Send & Tweak

  • FX-send track select: AUX → choose 1 or 2
  • Tweak reverb: AUX → FX track → turn knob
  • Launch Brain™ generator: AUX → choose Brain track

Instrument & Samples

  • Load synth/sample pack: INSTRUMENT + SHIFT + track button
  • Toggle keyboard velocity: COM → M1 → velocity toggle
