Step Editing

Basic Step Actions

  • Copy/Paste a step: → TAP
  • Nudge a step off-grid: Hold step → press L or R
  • Tie/extend note: Hold start & end steps → press SHIFT
  • Add step component: Hold step + component key

Pattern Controls

  • Set pattern length: HOLD BAR → SELECT desired last step → RELEASE
  • Step-record mode: Hold RECORD → enter steps
  • Live record mode: RECORD + PLAY → perform steps
